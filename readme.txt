Protect your valuable blog content(Pages & Posts) from getting copied.

= Features =
* **Disable right click** on your blog 
* **Disable selection of text** on your blog

You must enable the features from options page to protect your blog.

== Installation ==
For detailed Instructions go to plugins page at [Dynamic Blog Protector](http://111waystomakemoney.com/dynamic-blog-protector/)
1. Upload `blog-protector.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Go to **Settings** and then **Dynamic Blog Protector** to configure and protect your blog.
For detailed Instructions go to plugins page at [Dynamic Blog Protector](http://111waystomakemoney.com/dynamic-blog-protector/)
